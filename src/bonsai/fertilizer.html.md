# Fertilizer

I use a 1000 L rainwater tank topped up with municipal water when needed and
many of my trees currently have chlorosis. Here is a list of products I using
at the moment, delivered either daily from the watering hose connected to the
tank via a pump, or weekly from 3 L watering can or a 5 L sprayer:

## Nitrogen / Potassium (Organic Liquid Fertilizer)

- Product: [Engrais Liquide Organic Algoflash][1]
- Composition: NK 3-5.2 (derived from beet vinasse)
- Spray solution: 18 mL of product in 5 L of water (110 mg/L N, 155 mg/L K)
- Soil drench: 18 mL of product in 3 L of water (180 mg/L N, 260 mg/L K)

## Chelated Iron

- Product: [Anti-Chlorose Algoflash][2]
- Composition: Fe-EDDHA (6% Fe)
- Stock solution: 7 g of product in 0.5 L of water
- Spray solution: 9 mL of stock in 5 L of water (1.5 mg/L Fe)
- Tank solution: 1 g of product in 100 L of water (0.6 mg/L Fe)

## Calcium / Magnesium (Organic Liquid Supplement)

- Product: [Biobizz Calmag][3]
- Soil drench: 1.5 mL of product in 3 L of water - weekly
